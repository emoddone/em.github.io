Ghost Hands VR System

An innovative Unity-based VR training system that guides precise hand movements through ghosted visual feedback for medical education

Project Overview

Developed for medical training applications, this VR system addresses the critical challenge of teaching precise hand movements and techniques in a safe, repeatable virtual environment. Traditional medical training often relies on observation and verbal instruction, but complex procedures require spatial understanding and muscle memory that's difficult to convey through conventional methods.

The Ghost Hands system revolutionizes this approach by providing visual, spatial guidance that allows trainees to literally follow expert movements step-by-step. The solution has been successfully integrated into medical training curricula, improving learning outcomes and reducing the time needed to master complex procedures.

Key Features

Step-by-Step Guidance

Precise ghost hand positioning system that guides users through structured training sequences one step at a time

Interactive Trigger System

Game objects with interactable triggers that detect when users correctly match ghost hand positions

Ghost Handler Scripts

Custom scripts that manage step progression and integrate with dialogue menu systems for seamless user experience

Configurable Timing

Required time settings for each step's collider detection, ensuring users maintain proper positioning before advancing

Haptic Feedback System

Customizable haptic amplitude and duration settings for controller feedback when steps are completed correctly

AI-Generated Audio Cues

Integrated audio feedback system with AI-generated instructions to supplement visual ghost hand guidance
